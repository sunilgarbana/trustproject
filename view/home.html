The Subhash Chandra Bose Charitable Trust, established in 2025 is a non-profit organization founded by Mr. Narava. V. V. D. Prasad, a private lecturer in chemistry with a strong commitment to social development. Subhash Chandra Bose Charitable Trust aims to continue Subhash Chandra Bose's vision of a free, independent, and self-reliant India by supporting disabled individuals, orphans and social development. The trust main focus on Education employment and health care for disabled people and orphans. Adoption of beggers and utilizes them for cleaning environment and plantation. The Trust activities reflect Netaji's multifaceted contributions to Indian society.

Vision

The trust aims to continue Subhash Chandra Bose's vision of a free, independent, and self-reliant India by supporting disabled individuals, orphans and social development.

Mission

The trust main focus on

* Education employment and health care for disabled people and orphans.

* Adoption of beggers and utilize them for cleaning environment and plantation.
