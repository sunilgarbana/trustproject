Our History

Founder

Subhash Chandra Bose Charitable Trust was established in 2025 by Mr. Narava V. V. D. Prasad, a passionate chemistry lecturer with a deep concern for societal well-being. His inspiration stemmed from the ideals of Netaji Subhash Chandra Bose, whose legacy of courage, leadership, and service to the nation resonates in every initiative undertaken by the Trust.

Foundation & Growth

Initially started as a small initiative to support a few underprivileged children in Andhra Pradesh, the Trust rapidly expanded its outreach due to growing community involvement and support. Over the years, we have focused on key areas such as:

  • Education for rural and underserved children
  • Employment opportunities for disabled individuals
  • Health care access in remote areas
  • Environmental cleanliness and awareness
  • Orphan rehabilitation and welfare programs

Milestones

  • 2025: Establishment of the Trust and launch of first community outreach program.
